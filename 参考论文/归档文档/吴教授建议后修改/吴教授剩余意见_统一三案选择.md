### 方案 B

中文版：
这里采用 GraphRAG 而非 vanilla RAG 的原因在于，临床核验并不只是“找到相似文本”，而是要在诊断、用药、检验结果和时间信息之间恢复患者特定的关系结构。GraphRAG 能够把多表 EHR 中分散的证据组织为可查询的关系图谱，因此更适合支持句子级事实核验。

英文版：
Compared with vanilla RAG, which largely relies on matching flat text snippets, GraphRAG is better able to handle cross-table dependencies and multi-hop relations in medical records. Whether a discharge statement is correct often depends jointly on diagnosis tables, medication records, laboratory values, and temporal order; such dependencies are difficult to recover reliably through snippet retrieval alone, whereas graph-structured retrieval is better suited for this kind of patient-level evidence integration.

问题摘要：增强多智能体设计的学术理由，可引入 adversarial generation 或 LLM-as-a-judge 视角。

- 中文：[论文_md形式/03_related_work.md](论文_md形式/03_related_work.md)
- 英文：[论文_md形式/英文逐章稿/03_related_work.md](论文_md形式/英文逐章稿/03_related_work.md)

推荐方案：B

### 方案 A

中文版：
这一设计也与近期 LLM-as-a-judge 研究相呼应：在高风险评估任务中，让模型围绕证据进行结构化判断，往往比单次自由生成更接近可审计的质量控制流程。相应地，生成智能体与检测智能体的分工，使系统能够同时覆盖“构造挑战样本”和“执行证据核验”两类不同目标。

英文版：
This design is also consistent with recent LLM-as-a-judge research: in high-risk evaluation settings, evidence-grounded structured judgment is often closer to auditable quality control than a single free-form generation step. Correspondingly, separating the generation and detection agents allows the system to cover two distinct objectives: constructing challenging examples and performing evidence-based verification.

### 方案 B

中文版：
从方法论上看，CuraView 的双智能体设计结合了两条正在形成的技术脉络。一方面，对抗式样本生成能够持续构造更具迷惑性的错误，从而提升检测模块的鲁棒性；另一方面，LLM-as-a-judge 范式表明，围绕证据执行结构化评审比单次开放式回答更适合高风险质量控制场景。基于这两点，生成智能体与检测智能体的解耦并非单纯工程拆分，而是服务于“持续制造挑战样本”和“证据驱动核验判断”这两类互补任务。

英文版：
Methodologically, CuraView’s dual-agent design combines two emerging directions. On the one hand, adversarial sample generation continually constructs more challenging errors, improving detector robustness. On the other hand, the LLM-as-a-judge paradigm suggests that evidence-centered structured evaluation is better suited to high-risk quality control than a single open-ended response. Under this view, the separation between the generation agent and the detection agent is not merely an engineering decomposition, but a principled way to support two complementary tasks: producing hard cases and performing evidence-driven verification.

### 方案 C

中文版：
我们进一步将 CuraView 的多智能体架构理解为一种“对抗生成 + 证据评审”的联合范式。生成智能体负责系统化暴露检测系统的薄弱点，类似于面向鲁棒性训练的对抗样本构造；检测智能体则承担类似 LLM-as-a-judge 的职责，在限定证据上下文下输出可解释、可结构化的核验结论。相较于单模型同时负责生成与评审的方案，这种解耦更有利于减少目标混淆，并提升幻觉核验过程的可解释性与可审计性。

英文版：
We further interpret CuraView’s multi-agent architecture as a joint paradigm of adversarial generation and evidence-based judging. The generation agent systematically exposes weaknesses in the detector, analogous to adversarial example construction for robustness; the detection agent plays an LLM-as-a-judge role by producing interpretable and structured verification decisions under bounded evidence context. Compared with a single model handling both generation and evaluation, this decoupling better avoids objective conflation and improves the interpretability and auditability of hallucination verification.
